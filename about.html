Our Story

DRIVEN BY
PASSION

Excellence in Every Detail.

Located in the heart of Chennai, Dhuruv Detailing Studio was born from a singular obsession: to push automotive aesthetics to their absolute limits. We are not a parts shop; we are a premium facility dedicated to preserving, protecting, and perfecting elite automobiles.

We source only the highest grade materials globally—from self-healing paint protection films to ultra-durable ceramic matrix coatings and studio-grade audio systems. Every vehicle entrusted to us undergoes a meticulous transformation strictly governed by our uncompromising standard of excellence.

When you leave your keys with Dhuruv, you guarantee your vehicle will be treated with the highest caliber of respect and expertise in the industry.

Chennai's most premium automotive detailing studio. Where perfection meets passion, every single time.
